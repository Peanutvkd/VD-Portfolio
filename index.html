About Me

Hello! I am an aspiring engineer with an emphasis in robotics. I love learning about 3-dimensional design and code in my spare time. I am most proud of the website I created teaching students about drivers safety in California. I am also a strong advocate for women in STEM careers, participating in my school's Girls Build Club and Girls Who Code. In the future, I hopes to encourage women in STEM by pursuing an engineering degree. I want to revolutionize the world of automated technology and robotics. Specifically, I hope to participate in projects related to food delivery robots and autonomous technologies.
